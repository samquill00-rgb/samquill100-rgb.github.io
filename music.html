Points of Light (2012)

Points of Light was a collaborative project with Laurie Tompkins, David Feuters, Eoin Roe and Simmy Singh.
LISTEN / BUY HERE

Points Of Light have composed a serene and otherworldly suite where the David Tibet-like poetry and mannered vocals of Sam Quill are complemented by Eoin Roe's minimal keys and the mournful strings of Simmy Singh.

— Boomkat

“London Conversation” and “Points Of Light (fadeout)” could be said to be the two centrepiece cuts and undoubtedly make the strongest connection — they sway in unison on a base of stuttering beat and thump, with Sam Quill’s words paced carefully within and between each synthesiser surge.

— ATTN:MAGAZINE
